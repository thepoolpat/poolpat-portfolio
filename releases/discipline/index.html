Why This Album Exists

Discipline is the LP that pulled together two years of late-night sessions and verses traded across timezones. Built around heavyweight features — Shanakee and Farid Williams thread through almost every track — it's also the record where the remix of one cut hit #16 on Apple Music Ireland's Hip-Hop/Rap Albums chart.

Behind the Production

Influences sit on the record like fingerprints: the dense low-end of Massive Attack's Mezzanine, the wired-up chaos of Stupeflip, the wide-eyed scope of XXV-era compilations. Tracked between Cork sessions and remote stems from collaborators. Mixed for headphones first, then made to survive the car.
